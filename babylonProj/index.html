Rotation Scene

This scene uses lighting, imported textures, objects and rotations. 3 triangles on a plane peirce through a ring with a sphere in the center. Cubes rotate in place while one bounces off the sphere. Lighting has been used to place green on the top of the spinning cubes to give the illusion of grass.
